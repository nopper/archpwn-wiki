ArchPwn 2.0 RC1 Bootable USB.

Archpwn is easily installed to a bootable usb stick using UNetBootin. Available from the AUR here: [1]. Also most distributions have it in their repositories. Or available straight from their website here: [2]

  1. Using Gparted or other disk partitioner format the drive to Fat16 or 32.
  2. Re-mount the usb drive either by re-inserting it or via your file manager.
  3. Open unetbootin and provide your root password.
  4. Unetbootin should automatically detect your usb drive.
  5. Down at the DiskImage option click the ... button to browse for your ArchPwn image.
  6. Select OK and next and let unetbootin do its thing.
  7. When it is complete simply reboot your machine and boot into the ArchPwn live environment.
